The Story
Floristic began as a dream in 2023, sparked by a simple love for crafting beautiful things that make people smile. We started small, with just a handful of supplies and a passion for turning life’s little moments into memories. Every piece we created—whether an engagement hamper or a single bouquet—was crafted with the belief that the right gift could bring joy and make a lasting impression. In our first year, we poured our heart into each order, working closely with every client to ensure their vision became reality. By 2024, we had grown tremendously, receiving over 1,000 orders and expanding our offerings to include not just gifts but also custom wedding stationery and premium nikkah namas. Each creation became a symbol of Floristic’s dedication to quality, elegance, and personal touch. Our story is about more than just growth; it’s about connecting with people during their most special moments. From weddings to “just because” gifts, Floristic is here to help make every occasion unforgettable, one handcrafted piece at a time. Our journey is just beginning, and we can’t wait to see where it takes us—and you—next.
